`mytonctrl_bot` - This bot allows you to monitor the state of the validator.
To start using the bot, add the validator address to the watch list.
Instead of the full address of the validator, you can use the last few characters.

```
/add <adnl>     - Add a validator to your the watch list.
/del <adnl>     - Remove the validator from your the watch list.
/list           - Show the list of validators you are subscribed to.
/status         - Show the status of the validators you subscribe to.
/status <adnl>  - Show validator status at its address.
/status all     - Show status of all validators.
/status died    - Show status of dead validators
```
