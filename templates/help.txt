To start using the bot, add the node/validator address to the watch list by /subscribe_node command:

`/subscribe_node <NODE_ADNL_ADDRESS>`

You can get the ADNL address in MyTonCtrl with the `/status` command.

/status - Show the status of the validators you are subscribed to

/unsubscribe_node - Remove node from subscriptions.

/list_nodes - Show list of subscriptions.

/enable_alert - Enable notification for all nodes.

/disable_alert - Enable notification for all nodes.

/list_alerts - Show list of active notifications for the node.
