To start using the bot, add the node/validator address to the watch list by /subscribe\_node command:

`/subscribe_node <NODE_ADNL_ADDRESS>`

You can get the ADNL address in MyTonCtrl with the `status` command.

/status - Show the status of the validators you are subscribed to.

/unsubscribe\_node - Remove node from subscriptions.

/list\_nodes - Show list of subscriptions.

/enable\_alert - Enable notification for all nodes.

/disable\_alert - Enable notification for all nodes.

/list\_alerts - Show list of active notifications for the node.
