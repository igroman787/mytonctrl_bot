To start using the bot, add the node/validator address to the watch list by commad: `/subscribe_node <NODE_ADNL_ADDRESS>`

```
/status - Show the status of the validators you are subscribed to
/subscribe_node - Add ADNL address to subscriptions
/unsubscribe_node - Remove ADNL from subscriptions
/list_nodes - Show list of subscriptions
/enable_alert - Add warning check to your validators
/disable_alert - Remove warning check to your validators
/list_alerts - Show list of active warnings
```


**Example**:
Add ADNL address to subscriptions. Instead of the full address of the validator:
`/subscribe_node 1B4C7485C34EED9DD95E17F8032AD8E02FAD92264DE431AD8BDB5BE6AA194F59`
